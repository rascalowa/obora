MONIKA
Before she met her future husband horses was all her life. As a teenager she spended few hours every day in a stable. Taking part in the competition in dressage, show jumping and eventing was what she wanted to do all the time. Then she met Greg and he turned her life upside down. She started traveling with him and she learned how to kitesurf. Now she has big passion for strapless kitesurfing, but one day horses will come back to her life.
GREG
Sport always was present in his life from a young age. Especially he likes all kind of board sports. Nature enthusiast, his best way to relax is to feel the smell of the forest and the wind in his hair. In winter he loves to hit the backcountry, fresh light snow is the best for him. Summertime he switches his snowboard for surfboard, with or without a kite. Bigger wave = better day!
WANDERLUST

We had a plan to travel around the world but it turned out to be exhausting. Instead we have decided on taking shorter trips. With this idea we were able to enjoy every country we have visited, that is what we highly recommend! Work as a instructors while traveling helped us to stay afloat. Below we are presenting best places we have already visited. But you need to watch out: traveling is addictive!


1.
On the order between Agrentina and Brazil we can find Iguazu. Most amazing place we have seen so far, Monika's childhood dream. Iguazu is a set of 275 minor waterfalls, big mass of water in falling down every second (1700m2). Worth every penny!


2.
Wonderful beaches in Philippines. White sand, palm trees and amazing underwater world! Boracay's Whitebeach with r white sand and clear crystal water is great before/after highseason. Siargao island offers great waves for surfing and beautiful landscapes both on the coast and inland. Filipinos are very friendly and always willing to help. The best destination for longer holidays.


3.
Wild fauna and flora in CostaRica. Monkeys jumping above your head, huge sea turtles in the water and hunting pelicans are just small part of costarican wildlife. Deeper in the jungle you can find way more, but it is not always good idea to look for more.


4.
Canyon Colca in Peru. One of the deepest canyons in the world (3270m - twice as deep as Grand Canyon in the USA). Huge space between steep sides is a great place for biggest flying birds in the world. Condors weight up to 15 kg and their wingspan could be even 320cm! Just imagine these enormous animals flying few meters above your head!


5.
Salar de Uyuni in Bolivia is the world's largest salt flat (over 10,000 square kilometers). During dry season we can see hexagonal salt formations on the surface. Following rain Uyuni transforms into the world's biggest mirror. Unforgettable view...


6.
Whole wietnam is a beautiful country, but what we found the most interesting was the limestone formations in Ha Long Bay and in Tam Coc province. In Ha Long Bay we can see many islands on the water while Tam Coc has a very similar view but islands are between rice fields. Both of them are great.
